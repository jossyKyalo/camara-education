Completed Projects

Over the years, Camara Education has successfully completed numerous projects that have made a significant impact on education in various countries. These projects showcase our commitment to transforming learning through technology and our ability to deliver sustainable solutions.

iMlango Project - Kenya (2014-2020)

A comprehensive e-learning program that improved learning outcomes for over 150,000 marginalized girls in Kenya.

  • Location: Kenya (Kajiado, Kilifi, Makueni, and Uasin Gishu counties)
  • Partners: Department for International Development (DFID), Avanti Communications, sQuid, Whizz Education
  • Achievements:
  • Established computer labs in 205 primary schools
  • Trained over 5,000 teachers in ICT skills
  • Improved attendance rates by 15%
  • Increased literacy and numeracy scores by 25%

Digital Schools of Tanzania (2015-2019)

A project to digitize the Tanzanian secondary school curriculum and provide teachers with the skills to effectively use digital content in teaching.

  • Location: Tanzania (Dar es Salaam, Mwanza, and Arusha regions)
  • Partners: Tanzanian Ministry of Education, Dell, Microsoft
  • Achievements:
  • Digitized 75% of the secondary school curriculum
  • Established 80 digital learning centers
  • Trained 2,000 teachers in ICT-enhanced teaching methods
  • Reached over 100,000 students

ICT Policy Development - Zambia (2016-2018)

A project to support the Zambian Ministry of Education in developing a comprehensive ICT in Education policy and implementation plan.

  • Location: Zambia (nationwide)
  • Partners: Zambian Ministry of Education, UNESCO, World Bank
  • Achievements:
  • Developed a comprehensive ICT in Education policy
  • Created a detailed implementation plan
  • Trained 100 ministry officials in policy implementation
  • Established a monitoring and evaluation framework

Education Innovation Challenge - Ethiopia (2017-2020)

A project to identify, fund, and scale innovative educational technology solutions in Ethiopia.

  • Location: Ethiopia (Addis Ababa and Oromia region)
  • Partners: Ethiopian Ministry of Education, Google.org, Mastercard Foundation
  • Achievements:
  • Supported 25 educational technology startups
  • Provided over $1 million in funding
  • Mentored 100+ education entrepreneurs
  • Reached over 200,000 students with innovative learning solutions

Impact and Lessons Learned

Our past projects have had significant impact on education in various countries, and we have learned valuable lessons that inform our current and future work.

Key Impact Areas:

  • Improved access to quality education through technology
  • Enhanced teaching and learning processes
  • Increased student engagement and learning outcomes
  • Strengthened educational systems and policies
  • Empowered communities to take ownership of educational technology

Lessons Learned:

  • The importance of local ownership and community involvement
  • The need for comprehensive teacher training and ongoing support
  • The value of aligning technology initiatives with curriculum and educational goals
  • The importance of sustainability planning from the outset
  • The effectiveness of collaborative approaches involving multiple stakeholders
