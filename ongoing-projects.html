Ongoing Projects

Explore our current initiatives that are transforming education through technology.

Current Projects

Camara Education is currently working on several projects across Africa and Ireland. These projects represent our commitment to transforming education through technology and creating lasting impact in the communities we serve.

Digital Schools Program - Ethiopia

A comprehensive program to establish digital learning environments in 100 schools across Ethiopia, benefiting over 50,000 students.

  • Location: Ethiopia (Addis Ababa, Oromia, and Amhara regions)
  • Duration: 2023-2026
  • Partners: Ethiopian Ministry of Education, Dell Technologies, UNESCO
  • Impact: 100 schools, 2,000 teachers, 50,000 students

Teacher Digital Skills Initiative - Kenya

A comprehensive teacher training program focused on enhancing digital skills and pedagogical approaches for effective technology integration in education.

  • Location: Kenya (Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu counties)
  • Duration: 2024-2027
  • Partners: Kenyan Ministry of Education, Google for Education
  • Impact: 5,000 teachers trained, benefiting over 150,000 students

Digital Content Development - Tanzania

Creating culturally relevant digital content aligned with the Tanzanian curriculum, focusing on STEM subjects for primary and secondary education.

  • Location: Tanzania (nationwide)
  • Duration: 2023-2025
  • Partners: Tanzanian Institute of Education, Microsoft Education
  • Impact: Development of digital content for all STEM subjects, reaching 500,000 students

ICT in Education Policy Development - Zambia

Supporting the Zambian Ministry of Education in developing and implementing a comprehensive ICT in Education policy framework.

  • Location: Zambia (nationwide)
  • Duration: 2024-2026
  • Partners: Zambian Ministry of Education, World Bank, UNESCO
  • Impact: Development of a national ICT in Education policy and implementation plan

Project Monitoring and Evaluation

At Camara Education, we believe in rigorous monitoring and evaluation of our projects to ensure that they are achieving their intended outcomes and to continuously improve our approaches.

Our monitoring and evaluation framework includes:

  • Baseline Assessments: Conducted at the beginning of each project to establish initial conditions.
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous tracking of project activities, outputs, and outcomes.
  • Mid-term Evaluations: Conducted halfway through the project to assess progress and make necessary adjustments.
  • End-line Evaluations: Comprehensive assessment of the project's impact and achievements.
  • Impact Studies: In-depth research to understand the long-term effects of our interventions.

Through these processes, we ensure accountability, learn from our experiences, and demonstrate the value of our work to stakeholders.
