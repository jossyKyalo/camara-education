About Camara Education

Our journey, mission, and the impact we've created through technology-enabled education.

Who We Are

Camara Education is a pioneering, award-winning international charity, dedicated to transforming learning through technology since 2005. We believe that every child should have access to quality education enabled by technology, regardless of their background or location.

Our work spans across Africa and Ireland, where we provide schools with the resources, skills, and support needed to leverage technology for improved educational outcomes. Through our sustainable and holistic approach, we aim to create lasting change in educational systems globally.

Our Mission and Values

At Camara Education, our mission is to transform education using technology to improve learning outcomes for 20 million disadvantaged children by 2030. We are guided by a set of core values that shape everything we do:

Impact

We are committed to making a measurable, positive difference in the lives of children through education.

Innovation

We constantly seek innovative solutions to address educational challenges in underserved communities.

Sustainability

We create sustainable solutions that continue to benefit communities long after our initial intervention.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and work closely with communities, governments, and organizations.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and respect for the communities we serve.

Empowerment

We equip individuals with the skills and resources they need to take control of their own future.

Our History

Camara Education was founded in 2005 by Cormac Lynch, who was inspired by the potential of technology to transform education in disadvantaged communities. The organization began by refurbishing computers and providing them to schools in Africa.

Since then, we have grown into a global organization with operations in Ethiopia, Kenya, Tanzania, Zambia, Lesotho, and Ireland. We have established strong partnerships with governments, ministries of education, and other stakeholders to ensure the sustainability and impact of our work.

Over the years, we have received recognition for our innovative approach, including the UNESCO King Hamad Bin Isa Al-Khalifa Prize for the Use of ICT in Education.

Our Impact

Since our founding, Camara Education has made a significant impact on education globally:

  • Provided over 150,000 computers to schools
  • Trained more than 30,000 teachers
  • Reached over 3.5 million children
  • Established 10,000+ eLearning centers
  • Saved over 30,000 tons of e-waste from landfills
