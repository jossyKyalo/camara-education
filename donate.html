We accept donations of computers, laptops, tablets, and other hardware. Your donated equipment will be refurbished and used in schools across Africa and Ireland.

Partner with us to make a significant impact on education globally. We offer various partnership opportunities, from project funding to employee engagement programs.

Your Donation at Work

Every donation to Camara Education makes a real difference in the lives of children and teachers. Here's how your support is making an impact:

  • Over 3.5 million children reached with improved education
  • More than 30,000 teachers trained in ICT skills
  • 150,000+ computers provided to schools
  • 10,000+ eLearning centers established
  • 30,000+ tons of e-waste saved from landfills
