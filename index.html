Our Mission

At Camara Education, we believe that technology has the power to transform education and improve learning outcomes for all children. Our mission is to deliver real impact through technology by providing hardware, software, training, and support to schools across Africa and Ireland.

Since our founding in 2005, we have provided over 150,000 computers to schools, trained more than 30,000 teachers, and impacted the education of over 3.5 million children.

Our Activities

Camara Education is involved in a range of activities aimed at transforming learning through technology:

  • Training school teachers
