Our Activities

Discover how Camara Education is transforming learning through technology.

What We Do

At Camara Education, we engage in a variety of activities aimed at transforming education through technology. Our holistic approach ensures that we address all aspects of technology integration in education, from hardware provision to teacher training and policy development.

Hardware and Software Support

We provide schools with refurbished computers, tablets, and other hardware, along with educational software and content. Our approach involves:

  • Collecting and refurbishing donated computers
  • Installing educational software and content
  • Setting up computer labs and learning centers
  • Providing technical support and maintenance

Teacher Training

We believe that technology alone is not enough. Teachers need to be equipped with the skills to effectively integrate technology into their teaching. Our training programs include:

  • Basic digital literacy skills
  • Technology-enhanced teaching methods
  • Curriculum integration strategies
  • Assessment and evaluation techniques

Funding and Coaching

We provide funding and coaching to scale innovations in partnership with education ministries. Our support includes:

  • Grants for innovative educational technologies
  • Mentoring and coaching for education innovators
  • Capacity building for ministry officials
  • Support for pilot projects and scaling successful innovations

Strategic Policy Engagement

We work with governments and education ministries to develop policies that support technology integration in education. Our policy work includes:

  • Policy research and analysis
  • Development of policy frameworks for ICT in education
  • Advocacy for increased investment in educational technology
  • Support for the implementation of ICT in education policies

Our Approach

Camara Education follows a holistic approach to technology integration in education, ensuring that all aspects of the education system are addressed. We believe that technology is a tool, not a solution, and that its effective use depends on the people and systems that support it.

Our approach is based on the following principles:

  • Sustainability: We ensure that our interventions are sustainable by building local capacity and creating self-sustaining systems.
  • Partnership: We work in close partnership with local communities, schools, and governments to ensure ownership and relevance.
  • Quality: We prioritize quality over quantity, ensuring that our technology and training meet high standards.
  • Impact: We measure and evaluate our impact to continuously improve our work and demonstrate the value of technology in education.

Getting Involved

There are many ways to get involved with Camara Education and support our work:

  • Donate computers, tablets, or other hardware
  • Volunteer your time and skills
  • Make a financial donation to support our programs
  • Partner with us on projects and initiatives
  • Advocate for technology-enabled education
