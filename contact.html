Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to Camara Education?

You can make a donation through our website, by phone, or by mail. Visit our Donate page for more information.

Can I donate computer equipment?

Yes, we accept donations of computer equipment. Please contact us for more information on the types of equipment we accept and how to arrange a donation.

How can I volunteer with Camara Education?

We offer various volunteering opportunities, from technical support to teaching and mentoring. Please contact us or fill out the enquiry form to learn more.

How can my organization partner with Camara Education?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision of transforming education through technology. Please contact us to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
